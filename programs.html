Our Programs

Reaching children and families through the love of Christ

Flagship Initiatives

Discover how we're making a difference

Jesus' Children Community Outreach (JCCO)

Our annual flagship outreach bringing the Gospel and practical support to children in communities across Nigeria.

Frequency: Annual (August)
Target Age: 4-16 years
Locations: Rivers, Delta, Lagos
600+ children reached in 2024

School Outreach Initiatives

Partnering with schools for character development, moral instruction, and spiritual mentorship.

Frequency: Ongoing during term
Target Age: Primary & Secondary
6 schools visited

Partner Schools:

  • Destiny Int'l School
  • Excel Academy
  • Gracefield College
  • Heritage Schools
  • Royal Priory
  • Sunrise Academy

Children Prayer Conference (CPC)

A dedicated time of prayer, worship, and teaching to equip children in their spiritual walk.

Frequency: Quarterly
Target Age: 7-14 years
Theme: "Prayer Warriors"
250+ attendees in 2024

Student Leadership TEDx-Style Talks

Empowering students to speak, lead, and inspire their peers through leadership talks and workshops.

Frequency: Bi-annual
Target Age: 12-18 years
Format: 5-minute talks, panels
30+ student speakers trained

Parenting Talk-Out

Equipping parents with biblical principles and practical tools for raising godly children.

Frequency: Monthly sessions
Target: Parents & Guardians
Topics: Discipline, faith, education
150+ parents reached

Teens Empowerment Program

Mentorship, skill acquisition, and spiritual development for teenagers to discover their purpose.

Frequency: Weekly meetings + retreats
Target Age: 13-19 years
Focus: Leadership, career, faith
120+ teens active
