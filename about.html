About Uncle Jeremy Ministries

Transforming young hearts through God's love since 2021

Our Journey

From humble beginnings to touching thousands of lives across Nigeria

2021

First Edition Outreach - Rivers State

Launched with 187 children, establishing our foundational outreach model. Theme: "Jesus, the Light of the World".

2022

Second Edition - Delta State

Expanded to Delta State, reaching 300+ children. 150 children gave their lives to Christ. Theme: "Jesus, the Way Maker".

2023

Third Edition - Multi-State

Outreaches in Rivers, Delta, and Lagos States. Total children reached: 450+. Introduced school visitation program.

2024

Fourth Edition - Milestone Year

600+ children reached across multiple states. Launched Children Prayer Conference and Teens Empowerment Program.

Our Vision & Objectives

What drives us every day

Our Vision

"To establish the kingdom of God in young people, raising a generation of purpose-driven leaders who will transform their communities."

Spiritual Nurture

Leading children into a personal relationship with Jesus Christ through Bible teaching and discipleship.

Character Development

Instilling integrity, respect, and godly values that shape children into responsible adults.

Community Engagement

Mobilizing local communities to support and protect their children's future.

Leadership Training

Equipping young people with skills to become leaders in their schools and churches.

Family Support

Strengthening families through parenting programs and material assistance.

Evangelism

Sharing the Gospel through creative outreaches and partnerships with local churches.

Statement of Faith

What we believe

We believe in one God, eternally existing in three persons: Father, Son, and Holy Spirit.

We believe in the deity of our Lord Jesus Christ, His virgin birth, sinless life, miracles, vicarious and atoning death, bodily resurrection, ascension to the right hand of the Father, and personal return in power and glory.

We believe in the Holy Spirit, His personality, and His work in regeneration, sanctification, and empowerment for service.

We believe the Bible to be the inspired, infallible, and authoritative Word of God.

We believe that salvation is by grace through faith in Jesus Christ alone.

We believe in the spiritual unity of all believers in our Lord Jesus Christ.
